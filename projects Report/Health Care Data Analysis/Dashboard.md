# Health-Care Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Health-Care Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project built using **Power BI** to analyze healthcare data such as patient demographics, medical conditions, hospital admissions, billing amounts, and test results.

This dashboard provides insights into **patient distribution by gender, blood type, admission type, medical conditions, and yearly trends**. It helps healthcare organizations understand patient patterns and billing trends for better decision-making.

The project demonstrates how **data visualization and business intelligence tools** can transform healthcare datasets into meaningful insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze **patient distribution by gender and blood type**
* Study **yearly patient trends**
* Identify **common medical conditions**
* Evaluate **billing amounts by insurance providers**
* Analyze **hospital admission types**
* Understand **test results distribution**

# 📈 Dashboard Insights

## 1️⃣ Total Patients by Gender

The dashboard visualizes the distribution of patients by gender.

**Key Insight:**
Male and female patient counts are nearly equal, indicating a balanced gender distribution in the dataset.

---

## 2️⃣ Total Patients by Blood Type

The dashboard analyzes patient distribution across different blood groups such as:

* O+
* O-
* A+
* A-
* B+
* B-
* AB+
* AB-

**Key Insight:**
Certain blood types appear more frequently in the dataset, which can help healthcare providers manage blood bank resources.

---

## 3️⃣ Total Patients by Year

The line chart shows the **yearly trend of patient records**.

**Key Insight:**
Patient numbers increased between **2020 and 2023**, followed by a decline in 2024.

---

## 4️⃣ Billing Amount by Insurance Provider

The dashboard compares **total billing amounts across different insurance providers**.

Insurance providers include:

* Cigna
* Medicare
* Blue Cross
* UnitedHealth
* Aetna

**Key Insight:**
Insurance providers contribute significant portions of the overall healthcare billing.

---

## 5️⃣ Billing Amount by Medical Condition

The dashboard analyzes billing amounts based on medical conditions such as:

* Diabetes
* Obesity
* Arthritis
* Hypertension
* Asthma
* Cancer

**Key Insight:**
Chronic diseases contribute heavily to healthcare billing expenses.

---

## 6️⃣ Patients by Admission Type

The dashboard categorizes patients based on hospital admission type:

* Elective
* Urgent
* Emergency

**Key Insight:**
Emergency and urgent admissions make up a significant portion of hospital visits.

---

## 7️⃣ Patients by Test Results

The dashboard analyzes medical test outcomes:

* Normal
* Abnormal
* Inconclusive

**Key Insight:**
Most test results fall under normal outcomes, while abnormal and inconclusive results represent smaller proportions.
