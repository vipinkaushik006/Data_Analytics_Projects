AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard is a data analytics project developed using Power BI to analyze trends in the Artificial Intelligence job market.

This interactive dashboard provides insights into average salaries, job distribution across companies, required AI skills, and experience trends in the AI industry. The dashboard helps understand how AI skills and experience impact job opportunities and salary levels in major technology companies.

The project demonstrates how data visualization and business intelligence tools can be used to transform raw data into meaningful insights.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze average salary distribution across top technology companies

Identify the most demanded AI skills in the job market

Compare maximum salaries offered for different AI skills

Analyze job distribution among major companies

Study the relationship between experience and job opportunities

Build an interactive dashboard for AI job market insights
