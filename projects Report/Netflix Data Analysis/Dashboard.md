# Netflix / OTT Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Netflix / OTT Data Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project developed using **Power BI** to analyze streaming platform content such as movies and TV shows.

This dashboard provides insights into **content distribution by country, genre popularity, ratings, duration, and content types**. The objective of this project is to understand how OTT platforms distribute content across regions and which genres are most popular among audiences.

The project demonstrates the use of **data visualization and business intelligence tools** to transform entertainment data into meaningful insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze the **distribution of movies and TV shows**
* Identify **popular genres across OTT platforms**
* Study **content duration and rating trends**
* Analyze **content distribution across countries**
* Compare **movies vs TV shows**
* Build an **interactive OTT content analysis dashboard**

# 📈 Dashboard Insights

## 1️⃣ Movies vs TV Shows

The dashboard compares the number of **Movies and TV Shows** available on the platform.

**Key Insight:**
Movies slightly dominate the platform compared to TV shows, showing a larger content library of films.

---

## 2️⃣ Content Duration by Country

The dashboard visualizes the **total duration of content by country**.

Countries analyzed include:

* South Korea
* Canada
* Japan
* UK
* Spain

**Key Insight:**
Content production is distributed across multiple countries, indicating global content diversity.

---

## 3️⃣ Content Distribution by Genre

The dashboard shows the **count of titles by genre**.

Genres analyzed include:

* Comedy
* Romance
* Documentary
* Drama
* Thriller
* Horror
* Action
* Sci-Fi

**Key Insight:**
Comedy and Romance genres have the highest number of titles, indicating strong audience demand for these categories.

---

## 4️⃣ Rating by Category

The waterfall chart analyzes the **IMDB rating contribution by genre category**.

**Key Insight:**
Genres like **Drama, Thriller, and Action** contribute significantly to overall content ratings.

---

## 5️⃣ Content Distribution by Country (Map)

The dashboard includes a **geographical visualization** showing where content originates.

**Key Insight:**
OTT platforms provide content from multiple countries, highlighting the **global nature of streaming platforms**.

---

# 🎛️ Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes **interactive filters** that allow users to explore the data dynamically.

### Available Filters

* Release Year

These filters allow users to analyze **specific content trends based on year, region, or content type**.



# 📌 Key Takeaways

* OTT platforms provide a mix of **movies and TV shows**.

* Content production is distributed across **multiple countries globally**.
* Genre categories contribute differently to overall ratings.
* Interactive dashboards help analyze streaming data efficiently.
